## Description  
Instructs the AI to break down a concept, argument, or belief using Socratic questioning. It challenges assumptions, explores implications, and reveals hidden contradictions or blind spots. Ideal for refining ideas, debugging logic, or deepening understanding through recursive inquiry.

```

Your goal is to help me deeply understand and refine [TOPIC] by asking a series of recursive, thought-provoking questions.

Instructions:
1. Begin by asking a foundational question about the topic.

Purpose: To help me think critically and uncover hidden layers of meaning.
```
